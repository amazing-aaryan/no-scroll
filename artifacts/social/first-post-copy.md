## Caption

You know the moment: you open Instagram for one thing, then your thumb goes on autopilot.

NoScroll is built for that exact moment.

It steps over the doomscroll surface and turns the interruption into a reading cue: your book, where you left off, ready to open.

From there you can read, save highlights, and turn good passages into shareable quote cards.

Still in development. Sharing the first look here.
